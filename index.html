                                              Basics

                                              This is a virtual space I designed and created, from scratch, to keep thoughts and notes while working for quick and easy access

                                              It is almost an online notebook with a few hidden features for you to find...

                                              For any note / information saved, clicking once will allow you to copy it throughout the site

                                              As you use it more, you'll find more features and shortcuts naturally

                                              If you have any issues or come across anything not working as its supposed to, let me know

                                              I hope you enjoy using the site!

                                              - Rishi

                                              Logins

                                              A space where you can save your login details for quick and easy access

                                              Tap once: This will copy the information you click

                                              Tap twice: Clicking the platform value twice will move that login row to the Archive

                                              Once you are sure you don't want the login anymore, click the platform value twice in the Archive section to delete

                                              Notes

                                              A space where you can note down what you need to do or store information to refer to in the future

                                              You can create notes either in the Notes / To-Do, Meeting Notes or General Notes sections depending on your usage

                                              Tap Once: This will copy the note you clicked (It will maintain any line spaces you wrote originally)

                                              Tap Twice: Clicking the note twice will move it to the Archive

                                              Once you are sure you don't want the note anymore, click the note twice in the Archive section to delete

                                              Templates

                                              A space where you can keep templates of emails or sms messages you send often

                                              Tap Once: This will copy the template you clicked (It will maintain any line spaces you wrote originally)

                                              Tap Twice: Clicking the template twice will move it to the Archive

                                              Once you are sure you don't want the template anymore, click the template twice in the Archive section to delete

                                              Sections

                                              Sections are a feature I added with the ability to create sub-headings in the notes and templates categories

                                              They allow you to group notes and templates together and can be used repeatedly, even if you refresh the page or come back to it later

                                              If you are done working on that specific section / adding notes to it, you can double tap the section header to move all the notes to the archive

                                              If you accidently move it to the archive and want to recover it, simply double tap the section header again in the archive - we all make mistakes

                                              However, once you are sure you don't want the information in the section, in the archive, double tap each piece of info to delete it

                                              Once all the data within a section is deleted, the section header will also be deleted

                                              This provides a way of checking each piece of information before deleting, so that you avoid mistakenly deleting all items within a section

                                              The Archive

                                              The archives act as a backup on this site, allowing you to recover any data you might've accidentally deleted

                                              When you move a note to the archive, by default it will be copied on your device's clipboard so if you want to recover it instantly you can re-enter it in the relevant category

                                              However, if you realise that you want something you deleted a while ago such that it is no longer copied, it will be in the Archive until you choose to delete it permanently

                                              Tap Once: If you tap any information in the Archive once, it will be copied and you can re-enter it into the relevant category

                                              Tap Twice: If you tap any information in the Archive twice, you will permanently delete it

                                              Although, it is worth noting that to speed things up, if you are recovering multiple notes from the Archive, you can double tap it, which will copy and delete it, and then paste to add it back again

                                              However, make sure that you definitely don't want the note because as soon as you double tap it will be deleted permanently
